1. An inverted plaster relief of a crucified human body
2. A glass slide lightbox illuminating the relief from below
3. An Archimedes concave mirror flash, concentrating solar energy to produce the photothermal image

**The 7.1 Scalar Ratio:** The Vitruvian Man (Uomo Vitruviano, c.1490), when multiplied by scalar factor 7.1, produces the dimensional geometry of the Turin Shroud. This scalar is derived from Fibonacci compound proportion and is not coincidental.
